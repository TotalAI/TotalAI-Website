Roadmap

Short Term

Move TAI from Alpha to Beta:

  • Receive overall feedback on the direction/features of TAI from community.
  • Finalize Core Types and their methods.
  • Fix all major bugs/issues.
  • Add Tests.

Move TAI from Beta to Production:

  • Continue feedback loop with community.
  • Extensive testing and community use to remove all bugs.
  • Improve test coverage.

Long Term

The overall long term direction will be driven by the community needs/interests but there are a few known long term goals:

  • A Planner Type and Decider Type integration with ml-agents. (Deep RL)
  • A library of Type Packs for a diverse set of game types and AI types.
  • A library of Integrations for popular Unity Assets.
  • A library of Add-Ons for Unity Packages.
